Toronto Local Eats

Toronto is as diverse as a mega city can get! Of course my local must trys are from all across the globe.

  • Vagabondo! A local Italian resto in Downtown Toronto, with everything a hungry belly cold desire. Pasta, pizza and so much more, this affordable chic restaurant is a must visit, and will have you coming back for more.
  • Scotty Bons! This GTA Jamaican food chain, offers great bowls and sandwichs. Although this is not a dine in resturant it is a great take out spot, to enjoy on the go.
  • Mushkaki! This Somali-Canadian family run business, is a staple in toronto's Rexdale neighbourhood. Mushkaki offers traditional Somali rice and past meals, as well as deicious burgers and wraps.
